# Privacy Policy

**Itog — AI Meeting Notes for Mac**
Last updated: May 2026

## Overview

Itog is designed with privacy as a core principle. Your meetings are recorded and processed locally on your Mac. Audio never leaves your device.

## What We Collect

**We do not collect:**
- Audio recordings
- Transcripts
- Meeting protocols or notes
- Information about who you speak with
- Meeting content of any kind

All audio capture and transcription (via WhisperKit / Apple Neural Engine) happen entirely on your device. By default, AI summarization also runs locally through Ollama — no transcript data leaves your Mac. You can optionally connect a cloud AI provider in Settings (see Third-Party Services below).

**We may collect:**
- Anonymous crash reports (if you opt in via macOS crash reporting)
- App Store purchase receipts (processed by Apple, not us)

## iCloud Sync (Optional)

If you enable iCloud sync, your meeting protocols are stored in your personal iCloud account. This data is governed by [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/). We never have access to your iCloud data.

## Third-Party Services

**AI Summarization Providers (optional):** By default, Itog generates meeting protocols locally through [Ollama](https://ollama.com) running on your Mac — no transcript data leaves your device. You can optionally connect a cloud AI provider in Settings → AI Provider: Anthropic Claude, OpenAI, or OpenRouter. If you choose a cloud provider, your transcript (text only, not audio) is sent to that provider to generate the protocol. Please review their privacy policies: [Anthropic](https://www.anthropic.com/privacy), [OpenAI](https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy), [OpenRouter](https://openrouter.ai/privacy). Switch back to Ollama at any time to keep everything fully local.

**RevenueCat:** Used to manage in-app subscriptions. RevenueCat may collect purchase-related data. See [RevenueCat's Privacy Policy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy).

**Apple:** App Store purchases and crash reports are handled by Apple. See [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

## Data Storage

Meeting protocols are stored locally on your Mac in the app's sandboxed container. You can delete all data at any time from within the app or by uninstalling Itog.

## Children's Privacy

Itog is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Significant changes will be noted in the App Store release notes.
