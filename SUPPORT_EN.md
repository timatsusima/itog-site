# Support

**Itog — AI Meeting Notes for Mac**

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Does Itog join my calls as a bot?**
No. Itog records system audio and your microphone directly on your Mac. No bot joins your Zoom, Meet, or Teams call. Other participants cannot see or know that you are recording.

**Which meeting apps are supported?**
Any app that plays audio on your Mac: Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, FaceTime, Telegram, Discord, and more.

**Is my audio uploaded to the cloud?**
No. Transcription runs locally on your Mac via WhisperKit using Apple Neural Engine. Audio never leaves your device.

**Does the AI summary send my data to the cloud?**
By default, no. Itog generates meeting protocols locally through Ollama on your Mac — no data leaves your device. If you prefer a cloud AI provider, you can connect Anthropic Claude, OpenAI, or OpenRouter in Settings → AI Provider. In that case, only the transcript (text only, not audio) is sent to the chosen provider.

**How do I export my notes?**
Open History → select a meeting → click Export. Options include Markdown, PDF, Notion, and Telegram.

**The app doesn't capture system audio — what do I do?**
macOS requires explicit permission to capture system audio. Go to System Settings → Privacy & Security → Screen Recording → enable Itog. Then restart the app.

**Where is my data stored?**
All meeting protocols are stored locally in the app's sandboxed container on your Mac. You can enable iCloud sync in Settings for backup across devices.
